Wordpress guide

Getting Started

First you need to log in to the backend of your website.
I have created an account for each of you. You should have received an email from Wordpress containing your login information.

Log in

Once you have logged in, you'll be taken to your dashboard. From here you can update text, add new products and upload images.

Editing

Your Homepage

Starting from the top, here's how to add new photos to the image carousel:

  1. Navigate to the 'Carousel' link in the tool panel on the left of the dashboard screen.
  1. Click 'Add New'.
  2. Add a title (this just helps you to identify slides later).
  3. Then scroll down to 'Featured Image'. Here is where you need to add the image that will appear in the slider. Click 'Set featured image'.
  1. To upload a new image, select 'Upload Files' tab. Here you can either drag in an image file or select the files you'd like to upload.

When uploading a new image anywhere on the site, you need to ensure you fill in the 'Alt Text' field. This is the text that will describe the image to people who can't see them. It is vital this information is filled in and accurate - Google hates it if your images don't have alt text. Just click the image you are going to use and a side panel will appear containing information about the image. In the 'Alt Text' field you just need to input text that describes the image.

To optimise your website and make sure it runs as fast as possible, make sure you upload the correct image size. Uploading images that are too big will slow the site down. Uploading images that are too small will product images that look pixelated and blurred.

If you'd like to use an existing image, click to select the image and click 'Set Featured image'.

  1. Click publish and your new image will appear in the carousel.

To edit or delete an image in the carousel, just hover over title of the carousel image you'd like to change and you'll see the option to edit or delete it.

Edit Homepage Text

  1. Navigate to the 'Pages' link in your tool panel.
  1. Find the page titled 'Home' in the list of pages. Click 'edit'.
  1. Here you can edit the images and text as required. Use the visual editor to make your edits (you can switch to the html code view by clicking the 'text' tab, but unless you want to write code, I'd stick to the visual view)
  1. The tools in the visual editor are probably quite familiar to other text editing software you've used in the past, but the most useful tool here is the link button.
  • To create a new link, highlight the text you'd like to be the link and click the link button.
  • Type in your URL. If you are linking to another page on your website, start typing the title of the page and Wordpress will autofill it for you.
  • If you are linking to an external webpage, you want the link to open in a new tab. This is best practice and will improve the customer journey on your website. To add this function, click the cog on the right to bring up 'Link options'.
  • Click the checkbox next to 'Open link in a new tab'.
  • Finally, click update.
  1. To add a new image, click 'Add Media' at the top of the page. You'll be taken to the same media page you used to add a new testimonial. Click 'Upload Files', or select an existing image. As before, add alt text that describes the image and click 'Insert into page'.
  2. To edit an existing image, click on the image you'd like to edit and click the pencil icon. Edit the information as required and click 'Update'.
  1. Once you have made all your changes, click 'Update'.

Here is also where you can change the visibility of a page. If you'd like to keep the page, but unpublish it from the site, just click 'Edit' next the Status of the page and choose 'draft' from the dropdown menu.

The testimonials are pulled through from the testimonial plugin. To edit these, you need to edit Testimonials.

The 'Inspiration' section takes the two latest blog posts and displays it on the homepage. To edit these, you need to edit your Blog posts.

Editing and creating new

Testimonials

Here's how to add new testimonials to the testimonials slider on your homepage:

  1. First navigate to 'Testimonials' in the tool bar.
  1. Click 'Add New Testimonial'.
  2. Add a title (this is for your identification purposes only).
  3. Use the text editor to enter the body of the testimonial.
  4. Scroll down and fill out the fields in 'Testimonial information'. Only boxes that you enter information in will appear in your testimonial slider.

For continuity, I would recommend keeping the information you enter for each testimonial consistent. In your current site, I have just included the Client's name.

  1. Once you are happy, just click 'Publish/Update' and your testimonial will automatically be added to your testimonial slider.

How to edit an existing testimonial:

  1. Navigate to the Testimonial section, the same as above.
  2. Hover over the title of the testimonial you'd like to edit and select 'Edit'.

Here is also where you can quickly edit, delete and preview each testimonial.

  1. Now just edit the text, testimonial information as required and click 'Update'.

Editing and creating new

Blog Articles

Here's how to add new blog articles:

  1. First navigate to 'Posts' in the tool bar.
  1. Click 'Add New'.
  2. Add a title, this will be displayed in both the article page and on the homepage in the 'Inspiration' section.
  3. Use the text editor to enter the body of the blog article.
    When pasting text in from another text editor (like word or Pages) you need to ensure you either 'paste as plain text' or select 'paste and match formatting'. This will strip out existing formatting and prevent formattting issues on your website.
  4. Add an image to the blog (again this will appear in both the blog article and on your homepage) by clicking 'Set featured image'. Dimensions should be 417px wide by 479px high.
  5. Once you are happy, click 'Update'.
    If you would like to check how your blog will look before publishing it to the website, click 'Preview Changes'.

You can edit a blog post in exactly the same way that you'd edit a testimonial.

Editing and creating new

Products

Here's how to add new products:

  1. First navigate to 'Products' in the tool bar.
  1. Click 'Add Product'.
  1. Add a product title.
  2. In the first text box, add detailed information about the product. This will be diplayed beneath the image on the product page.
  1. Fill in the product data. Here you can decide how much or how little information you'd like to include for each product. As before, keep this consistent across all products.
    To get you started, I've included the following for each product I've uploaded so far:
    • Price - you just need to add the digits. The site will add the currency symbol.
      If you add a sale price, it will show both the new price and old price with a 'sale' banner on the product.
    • In the inventory tab, you can set the stock status of the product. Here you can be as detailed as you need to be. I've just set that the product is in stock. You'll see more options if you tick the 'Manage stock?' checkbox.
  2. Add a product description. This will appear on the product page alongside the image. This will be the first thing your visitors read about the product so make it good!
  1. If you have one, you can add a review for the product. You could add an image of the product being used at an event. To do this, just click the 'Add comment' button at the bottom of the page. The review will appear in the tab alongside the product detail section.
    Visitors can also add their own reviews, but these will not display until they have been approved.
  1. Add a category to your product. If you don't add a category then it won't appear on the website.
    Products can have multiple categories - it just means they will appear in multiple places across the site. I would suggest adding all categories that apply to each product - giving it maximum exposure.
  1. Add an image to the product by clicking 'Set product image'.
    If you'd like to display multiple images for a product, you can also 'Add product gallery images'. This will create a slideshow of images to help you fully showcase the product.

You can edit a product in the usual way by clicking the title or hovering on the title and selecting the edit option. You'll notice that you also have the option to duplicate a product. I would suggest using this where possible as it will help you to maintain continuity throughout the site.
