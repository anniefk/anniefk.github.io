Getting Started

First you need to log in to the backend of your website.
I have created an account for you. You should have received an email from Wordpress containing your login information.

Log in

Once you have logged in, you'll be taken to your dashboard. From here you can update text, add a new wedding to your portfolio and upload images.

Editing

Your Homepage

Starting from the top, here's how to add new photos to the image carousel:

  1. Navigate to the 'Appearance' tab in the tool panel on the left of the dashboard screen.
  1. Next, click 'Theme options' from the drop down menu.
  2. Now choose Home Slider Settings
  3. Click 'Add Slide'
  4. Click on the title of the new slide, 'New Slide'. The panel will extend to show additional options. Cick 'Upload'.
  5. From here you will see the image library. You can either click on an existing image or click 'Upload Files' to add a new file.

    In order to ensure that images appear correcly in the slider, you need to make sure the image is 1170px wide by 657px height

  6. When uploading a new image anywhere on the site, you need to ensure you fill in the 'Alt Text' field. This is the text that will describe the image to people who can't see them. It is vital this information is filled in and accurate - Google hates it if your images don't have alt text. Just click the image you are going to use and a side panel will appear containing information about the image. In the 'Alt Text' field you just need to input text that describes the image.

    To optimise your website and make sure it runs as fast as possible, make sure you upload the correct image size. Uploading images that are too big will slow the site down.
    Uploading images that are too small will product images that look pixelated and blurred.

  7. Click 'Select' and your image will be added to a new slide
  8. Add a title to the image (this will appear on the slide in the uppercase text
  9. Add a description to the image, this will appear in the cursive text on the slide
  10. Click 'Save Changes' and the new slider will appear on the homepage.

To edit or delete an image in the carousel, just click on the title of the slider item, edit the image or text and click 'Save Changes'.

Editing and creating new

Testimonials

Here's how to add new testimonials to the testimonials slider on your homepage:

  1. First navigate to 'Testimonials' in the tool bar.
  1. Click 'Add New Testimonial'.
  2. Add a title (this is for your identification purposes only).
  3. Use the text editor to enter the body of the testimonial. Ensure the 'Visual' tab is selected. The tools in the visual editor are probably quite familiar to other text editing software you've used in the past.
  4. Scroll down and fill out the fields in 'Testimonial information'. Only boxes that you enter information in will appear in your testimonial slider.

For continuity, I would recommend keeping the information you enter for each testimonial consistent. In your current site, I have just included the Client's name.

  1. Once you are happy, just click 'Publish/Update' and your testimonial will automatically be added to your testimonial slider.

How to edit an existing testimonial:

  1. Navigate to the Testimonial section, the same as above.
  2. Hover over the title of the testimonial you'd like to edit and select 'Edit'.

Here is also where you can quickly edit, delete and preview each testimonial.

  1. Now just edit the text, testimonial information as required and click 'Update'.

Editing

Services and About pages

Here's how to edit your Services and About page:

  1. First navigate to 'Pages' in the tool bar.
  2. Select the page you would like to edit by either clicking on the title, or if you hover over the title you'll see several options to 'Edit', 'Quick Edit', 'Bin' and 'View'.
  3. Now you can edit the text using the visual editor as before.
  4. Once you have finished editing, simply click 'Update' and your changes will be saved.

Editing and creating new

Gallery items

Here's how to add a new wedding to the gallery:

  1. First navigate to 'Portfolio' in the tool bar.
  2. Click 'Add New'.
  3. Add a title - this will be displayed both on the gallery menu page and the individual page.
  4. Using the visual editor as before, write a brief summary of the wedding, this will appear underneath in the photos, as below:
  5. If you'd like to add a testimonial (you need to already have created it in the testimonials plugin) click the 'testimonials' dropdown.
  6. Select 'Single testimonial'
  7. Select the title of the testimonial you'd like to display from the drop down menu.
  8. Set the theme as 'No Style' and leave the width blank. Click 'Insert Now' and a line of cde will be added to the text field.
  9. Scroll down until you get to the 'Portfolio Post Options'. In the 'Project Layout' option, select 'Three Rows'
  10. Choose 'Image Grid' from 'Project Options'
  11. And select 'Three Column' from the Column drop down. Leave the Slider height and width fields empty.
  12. In the Value 01 Title field add the text 'Wedding Theme:'' and in the field below add the theme for the wedding.
    Fill in the fields as below. Any fields you don't fill in won't appear on the page.
  13. In the Slider Images, click 'Add/Edit Gallery'
  14. In the Image Gallery that pops up, click 'Add to Gallery'.
  15. Select the images you'd like to add, not forgetting to add 'Alt text' if the you upload new images and click 'Add to gallery'.
    Always add either three, six or nine images as this is the most effective.
  16. Finally, you need to 'Set featured image'. This will be the image displayed on the main gallery page.
  17. Click 'Publish' and your new gallery item will be visible on the gallery page.
