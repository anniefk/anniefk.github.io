Project Summary

Client: Amplify Media Partners Ltd
Finished Project: Design and build website
Date: May 2019

The Brief

Amplify Media Partners Ltd. needed a website to showcase their new venture. They had a logo and a rough idea of colour schemes, the rest was up to me.

The website needed to be easy to maintain and update and it needed to be responsive.

The Project

I designed a single scroll website that separated content using bold colours and imagery. The use of the colour blue was one of the key requirements and so this is a primary colour used throughout.

I built the website in Wordpress with a custom theme to enable the client to easily update and maintain the site.
