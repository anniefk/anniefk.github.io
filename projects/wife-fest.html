Wife-Fest

Project Summary

Client: My wife
Finished Project: Wedding website, Invitation, Personalised stickers and Temporary tattoos
Date: June 2017

The Brief

Possibly the hardest brief I have ever had to meet as the customer in this project was myself and my wife. We wanted a website to display all the information we needed our guests to know ahead of our big day. It needed to be easy to use (for our older guests) but still fun, informative and a reflection of the wedding we were planning.

To save costs we decided to email our Save the Dates to our guests.

We also needed an invitation that would tie in with the website and direct our guests to RSVP online.

Everything created digitally had to be responsive as we knew that a large number of our guests would be viewing the information on their phones

The Project

I created a single page website that had a fun introduction to our wedding party, information about the venue, gifts, a countdown timer and the all important RSVP form.

To inject a touch of fun, and convey that the wedding was not going to be normal traditional 'do', I added quirky illustrations inspired by our venue and theme.

I carried the theme through to the invite to create continuity. Elements from the website and invite even cropped up on our big day as I used the same illustrations and fonts for our personalised stickers and temporary tattoos!
