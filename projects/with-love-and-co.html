With Love & Co.

Project Summary

Client: With Love & Co.
Finished Project: Branding and Website
Date: June 2016

The Brief

As a start-up, With Love & Co. just had a presence on Social Media when they approached me. They needed a website to showcase their products and help to translate their current branding into digital.

The company provide a full service events solution, from invitation designs, to entertainment and decor hire. As a highly dedicated due, the two directors needed a modern and stylish website that would allow them to easily add new products and easily reply to customer enquiries.

The Project

Since the requirement to be able to easily add and remove products depending on stock, I created With Love & Co. a bespoke Wordpress website. Using Wordpress meant that the pair could easily update the site using the easy-to-use CMS, whilst still giving me the freedom to create them a bespoke website that reflected their brand voice.

The final product is a slick and modern looking website, with clear stock pages and an easy to use contact form.
